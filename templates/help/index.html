Quick Tips

  • Login: Use your email address to log in, not your username.
  • Reports: Reports can only be created for active tenants with complete configuration.
  • Schedule: Use cron expressions to schedule reports (e.g., 0 9 * * 1 for Monday 9 AM).
  • Test: Always test your report configuration before enabling scheduled delivery.
